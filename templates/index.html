A quick rundown of the site:

Activity Log(top of the app)

The Activity Log periodically checks the server for updates, such as a fellow guildmate logging a kill on an MvP, and displays the new activity at the top of its log.

Quick Summary(left side of the app)

Displays the name of every MvP in the database and its current status(alive, dead, etc). Features a search bar to quickly filter through the mvps.

Core app

Each MvP entry in the database has its own block in the app. The blocks include a picture of the MvP, its name, the field it spawns in, the time it takes to respawn,
and its current status(e.g dead, alive). If the MvP is not alive, a timer will be displayed counting down. Once the timer has counted down to zero, the MvP has guaranteed
respawned since its last recorded death. Due to the nature of how MvPs respawn in Ragnarok Online, there is no fixed amount of time most MvPs respawn.
Instead, most MvPs respawn between X and Y minutes after it has been killed. When the timer has counted down to the MvP's "window" of respawning, the background of the
MvP's block will turn orange and an audio queue will be played.
